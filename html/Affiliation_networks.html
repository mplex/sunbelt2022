Bipartite plots

A depiction of affiliation networks is found in bipartite graphs, which in this case is a clustered bipartite graph.

bmgraph(G20, layout="bipc", clu=list(ac, ec), cex=3)

Bipartite graph of G20 with clustering information

Moreover, a graph of the two-mode data with a binomial projection can use a stress or a force directed layout.

bmgraph(G20, layout="force", seed=1, vcol=8, cex=3, fsize=8)

Graph of G20 with a force directed layout

Formal concept analysis

An algebraic approach for the study of affiliation networks is found in Formal concept analysis.

Galois connections

The analysis of cross domain relations is with Galois connections.
